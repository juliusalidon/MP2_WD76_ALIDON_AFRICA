ABOUT

Welcome to Cream Puffs by MS, where the delicate art of pastry-making meets the irresistible allure of cream puffs. Our journey began with a passion for crafting exquisite desserts that not only satisfy cravings but also awaken a sense of pure delight in every bite.

Founded in 2017, Cream Puffs by MS was born out of a deep love for the delicate balance between flavors, textures, and aesthetics. Our founder, Myla Sincioco, a seasoned pastry chef with a penchant for innovation, embarked on a mission to redefine the cream puff experience. Drawing inspiration from both traditional recipes and contemporary culinary trends, Myla Sincioco envisioned a haven where cream puffs would transcend the ordinary and become a canvas for edible art.




MISSION

To be more than just a pastry shop - to be a conduit of happiness. We aspire to elevate the cream puff experience by consistently delivering unparalleled quality, fostering an environment of creativity, and nurturing connections through the love of desserts. Each cream puff represents our commitment to turning ordinary moments into extraordinary memories.

Join us in celebrating the artistry of cream puffs at Cream Puffs by MS. As we continue to evolve, we remain steadfast in our dedication to crafting dreams, one cream puff at a time.
